Macbook Neo is literally macbook m1 without touchID, true tone, and backlit keyboard. It has smaller screen size and older USB-C versions (M1 has Thunderbolts/UBS4, Neo has USB3 and USB2). 

## So what is okay about new Macbook Neo? 

The price, especially for students. In Poland we can get it for 2499zl(499$) as a students and for 2999zl(599$) for the others. 
The other good thing is that it has newer chip so even if it has similar performance to old M1 it will have longer support. 
So at the end of the day we can say that this laptop is great for students that doesn't care about such a small details and doesn't need big performance to render video, 3D graphics or train AI models. 
It's cheap, silent, small and lightweight, power efficient and you can do everything what macbook can offer you. 
Personally I would recommend to sacrifice some time for finding used mac m3 or m2 in similar price, but if someone just wanna have new product then Neo is okay.
