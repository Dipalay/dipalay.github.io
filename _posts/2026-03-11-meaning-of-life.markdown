# Planning is easy
Sometimes we wanna start being productive, change our life, start some projects, just become better version of ourselfes by doing some stuff that matters regularly. Then we start planning our daily basis. Every single day of week is fullfilled with activities we wanna do to make something good and making these plans is fucking enjoyable and easy. But what about the reality? Reality is often brutal - after some time we usuallly go back to the old habits, becaue too often it was impossible to follow our dayplan. 

# Is it possible to make plan that will mostly work for long time?
I think that we can't make plan that will work for most of our life. We need to adjust our plans regularly to our situation. We should make priorities list and just resign from things that are less important when we can't do everything, cause of unexpected events. Of course we can do some very general plan list for a longer amount of time - instead of doing plan of a day, we can plan what we wanna do in some window of time. For example, instead of wrting that I will go to gym in wednesday at 5PM, just write that I will go to gym at least onace a weekend. Of course it's good to have planned everything perfectly but sometimes it's good to make few versions of our plan in case of dealing with unexpected events. I think best option is to make detailed plan, general plan, and you can also make some to do list for important and optional things. And like I said you will probably need to change your plan from time to time to adhust it to current life events that are regularly changing.

# We are fucked
Planning is something for me that is really dopamine generating activity. I'm often excited about things that I want to do, but usually I'm not determined enough to focus to the plan and I'm getting back to brain rotting and overthinking instead of creating something that will exist as a new thing in our universe (like this text). To be honest it's trivial, but fucking true - just taking action is only thing that will make any impact on the world and overthinking and overplanning is the bigest enemy, cause we are making big plans, big hopes and we analyzing possible future, possible situations of what we can do, but usually it's worth nothing, cause we are not able to even start taking action to make it real. I hope that it will change, but our world is making us so overstimulated that we are lazier than any other generation before, so we are fucked.
